Welcome to Xandaria,

a website made for space lovers. (I am currently redoing the optimization of this website as it has a few displaying issues on the Incoming space launches page and the Astronauts page.)

ISS Live tracker:

This is the main feature of the website, a map tracking the live position of the International Space Station (ISS). After a few seconds to let the data load, you will be able to see the path the ISS took in the past 45 minutes and the path it will take in the next 45 minutes.

Incoming space launches:

This page will display a countdown adapted to your timezone of the 10 next space launches. You can also learn about each specific launch by hovering over the description button if you are on a computer or by clicking the button if you are on anything else.

The Astronomy picture of the day:

On this page you will be able to find the picture of the day and its description written by an astronomer. The content will update itself everyday through the NASA API, make sure to come every day to learn something new!

Astronauts:

You can on this page see a list of the astronauts currently in mission on the ISS containing each of their name, their picture and also a quick description.
